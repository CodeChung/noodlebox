WHY NOODLEBOX?

The demand for fresh, easily customizable food is rapidly growing and Noodlebox has been a trusted name in this business for over 15 years. With average annual store sales in excess of $1,000,000, the Noodlebox brand is a key player in the rapidly growing fast casual dining category.

NOT A TREND, IT'S WHO WE ARE

Sustainability and transparency in our food production is not just a trend for Noodlebox, it's the cornerstones of our business, and at the heart of who we are. This makes Noodlebox perfectly positioned to service an exciting new generation of customers that are prioritizing a curated menu made with premium products, with trusted transparency over low prices.

SERVING THOSE WHO ARE UNDER SERVED

We make all our dishes fresh when ordered, which means we can easily accommodate many dietary restrictions, providing options for people who typically have very limited options when dining out.

THE COMPLETE PACKAGE

Noodlebox provides a complete dining experience that lives and breathes its values. Starting with our 'guests in our home' service model that makes sure everyone feels welcome, comfortable & relaxed, to our open concept kitchen that invites you to watch our chefs cook your meal in our firery theatre, with fresh ingredients and house made sauces. Our modern, vibrant and energetic store experience was designed with sustainability in mind. You will find retired woks repurposed as dynamic light fixtures, and all our food is served in either reusable, recyclable or compostable dishes and utensils.

SUPPORT

Your success is our success, so we partner with you every step of the way. From site selection, store design, and training to ongoing operational and marketing support. Our systems are designed to put your success within reach.

FRANCHISE COSTS

If you have a passion for real food, made fresh with fire, here are the costs to open a Noodlebox franchise.

  • Initial franchise fee: $35 000
  • Unencumbered capital required: approx. $200,000
  • Total start up costs: $500 000 - $600 000
  • Royalty Fee: 6%
  • Advertising Fee: 1.5%
